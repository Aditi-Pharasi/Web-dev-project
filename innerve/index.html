Greetings to all the techies out there! We welcome you to the platform where we introduce you to an exciting journey of techies in the year 2023! Innerve is the annual technical fest of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW). With its inception in 2015, every year, the two-day extravaganza has witnessed a phenomenol gathering of students from all domains. The event is back for its FIFTH edition where it aims to be a platform for participants to test their engineering skills and showcase their prowess in the VIRTUAL mode.
